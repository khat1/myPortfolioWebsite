Banking System Development

Python was my very first programming language. It helped to lay the foundations into the programming world. The first project I worked on was the development of an Banking System. I explored and practiced many programming concepts and developed not only a text based system but a very old fashioned GUI.

Innovation Fest: E-bin

Collaborating in a group to solve a real-life problem. E-bin was an idea which helped to tackle problems faced with recycling. The E-bin consisted of an automated recycling system which separated the materials into the different bin components through the object recognition algorithms. Python in-depth and circuit building was explored throughout the project as well as time management and meeting the objectives to complete the project.

Smart Sight

The aim of the project was to increase accessibility of reading. For people who have visualimpairment or blindness through creating a device which can be worn by the user on theirday to day life. This will also have an direct effect and influence their independence whetherit is reading at home or at a workplace. The device was designed to be portable meaning itcan be taken and used anywhere and anytime.
